How To Play GTT

  • Step one: Click on "settings", Scroll To "Make A Deposit" And Make A Deposit With Minimum Of #500
  • Step Two: Click on "settings", Scroll To "Availability Settings"
  • Step Three: Select Any Game Of Your Choice
  • Step Four: Input Stake Amount Of Your Choice; Minimum Stake #200

  • Step Five: Click On Make Me Available

  • Step Six: Challenge Or Accept Challenge From Any Available Player With Game And Stake Amount Of Your Choice
